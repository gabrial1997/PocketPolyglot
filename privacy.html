Privacy Policy

This page explains what information PocketPolyglot collects, why, and how you can control or remove it. It applies to the PocketPolyglot iOS app.

What we collect

  • Account email — used to sign you in and identify your account.
  • Learning progress — which words and phrases you've practiced, how well, and when, so the app can schedule reviews and show your coverage of everyday Latvian speech.
  • Voice recordings — only if you explicitly turn on recording consent in the app. Nothing is recorded without that in-app consent.

Voice recordings

If you enable recording, your speaking attempts are stored privately and kept over time so you and the app can see how your pronunciation is progressing. A human reviewer on our side may listen to a sample of recordings to help us improve the app. Recordings are never shared publicly.

There is a separate, optional consent for using recordings to help train pronunciation models. This is off by default and only used if you turn it on separately from recording consent itself.

Where data lives

PocketPolyglot uses Supabase as its data processor. Your account data, learning progress, and any voice recordings are hosted in Supabase's EU region (Frankfurt, Germany).

Your rights (GDPR)

You can exercise your rights directly in the app, no request needed:

  • Delete recordings — remove your voice recordings at any time from Settings → Profile.
  • Delete your account entirely — also available from Settings → Profile. This removes your account, learning progress, and recordings permanently.

Both actions are permanent and remove the underlying data — they are not just hidden from view. You can also contact us (see below) if you'd like help with a data request.

Retention

Your data is deleted when you delete your account. Voice recordings can also be deleted independently, at any time, without deleting your account.
