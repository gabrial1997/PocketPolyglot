PocketPolyglot

Support & FAQ

PocketPolyglot is a Latvian learning app built around the first 1,000 most common words in everyday Latvian speech. You practice each word and phrase three ways — hearing it, choosing it among options, and saying it aloud — so recognition and production build together as your coverage of everyday speech grows.

Frequently asked questions

Consent and recording

Recording your voice is optional and off by default. You can turn it on or off at any time from Settings → Profile. Nothing is recorded unless you've explicitly enabled it.

Deleting your data

From Settings → Profile you can delete your voice recordings on their own, or delete your entire account (which removes your recordings and learning progress along with it). Both actions are permanent.

Why is the Listen tab locked?

Podcasts unlock once you can follow 25% of everyday speech. The lock isn't a paywall — it's there because listening to native-speed Latvian podcasts is much more useful, and much less discouraging, once you have enough vocabulary coverage to follow along.

Reporting a bug

Tap the 🐞 button inside the app at any time to send a bug report, along with a screenshot of what you were seeing.
